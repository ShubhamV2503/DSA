
1. Hashmap : 


a =  [5,5,4,4,6,6,4,4]

hashmap = {}

for i in a:
    if i not in hashmap:
        hashmap[i]  = 1
    else:
        hashmap[i] = hashmap[i]  + 1


data = ['apple', 'banana', 'apple', 'orange', 'banana', 'apple']

freq = {}

for item in data:
    freq[item] = freq.get(item, 0) + 1

2. XOR :


XOR of two same binary number  1^1 = 0
XOR of two different binary number 1^0 = 1

XOR of two same number:  5^5 = binary of 5 is  : 101 so 101 ^ 101 = 0
XOR of two different number:  5^4 = binary of 5 and 4 is  : 101 and  100 : == 101 ^ 100 = 001
